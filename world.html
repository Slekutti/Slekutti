About World

Shrouded in a veil of perpetual night, the Slekutti reside on a man-made land known as South Perlin. Originally elite soldiers in the service of Asmodeus, they now live in relative peace, free to choose their own path and become whoever they wish to be.

South Perlin is home to many significant and notable locations, each holding historical and cultural importance tied to the Slekutti’s heritage and the island’s unique geography. For more details on these locations, you can consult the Travel Brochure, which offers valuable insights and helpful tidbits!
